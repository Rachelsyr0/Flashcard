Guide to your first hour in 'Animal Crossing'

About 'Animal Crossing'

After nearly two years of hype, "Animal Crossing: New Horizons" — the latest game in Nintendo's adorable life-simulator series, which centers on the player character starting a new life on a deserted island — launched on Friday, and has already won the hearts of gamers around the world. Here's everything you need to know to get started in "Animal Crossing: New Horizons," including how to best pay off your first loan, and even build a house.
